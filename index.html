About Me

Hello, Universe! 🌌

I'm Christopher Jho S. Ledesma — a passionate and purpose-driven UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer currently studying Computer Science at the University of Nueva Caceres. With a strong eye for aesthetics and a mind wired for a clean code, I thrive on turning creative concepts into meaningful, user-centered web experiences.

My primary focus lies in designing responsive, intuitive interfaces that blend modern design with functional logic. I particularly enjoy building educational platforms, interactive portfolios, and role-playing websites that help fellow students—especially in the ICT track—discover and navigate their learning journeys with confidence and clarity. Each project I take on is crafted with care, using tools like Figma for design and VS Code for development.

I specialize in integrating subtle animations, parallax motion, and engaging layouts that bring websites to life—without compromising accessibility or performance. I believe great design should not only look beautiful, but also feel effortless and inclusive to use, across all screen sizes and devices.
